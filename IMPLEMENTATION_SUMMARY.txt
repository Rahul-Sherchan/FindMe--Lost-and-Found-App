🔍 ACTIVITY SCORING FORMULA
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Component Weights:
  New Users × 2
  Posts × 3
  Recovered Items × 5  (Most important)
  Messages × 1

Example Calculation:
  
  3 users + 7 posts + 2 recovered + 12 messages
  = (3 × 2) + (7 × 3) + (2 × 5) + (12 × 1)
  = 6 + 21 + 10 + 12
  = 49 points
  = 49% Activity = MEDIUM (Yellow)

💾 DATABASE QUERIES (Optimized)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Efficiently retrieves:

  • New users per date
    User.objects.filter(date_joined__date=target_date).count()

  • Lost/found posts per date
    Item.objects.filter(created_at__date=target_date, item_type='lost').count()

  • Recovered items per date
    Item.objects.filter(Q(status='resolved')|Q(status='claimed'), 
                       updated_at__date=target_date).count()

  • Messages per date
    Message.objects.filter(created_at__date=target_date).count()

All using Django ORM (SQL injection safe) with proper filtering!
